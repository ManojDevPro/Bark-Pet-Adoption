Malli's tale unfolds with heartbreak after she was found abandoned in a cardboard box on the streets. She had been involved in a terrible accident that had damaged her forelimb beyond repair, and sadly, she would require amputation surgery. Having suffered so much for one so young, once safe under our care and having undergone lifesaving surgery, we were devastated when she fell sick with the potentially deadly disease: parvo. Thankfully, this sweet wee soul had something of a warrior spirit within her, and after a bravely fought battle with abandonment, loneliness, pain, and disease, she came through it all to emerge victorious!
