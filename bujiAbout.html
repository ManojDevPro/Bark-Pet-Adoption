Buji's often scavenge for food and rarely find consistent, nutritious meals. They struggle to access clean water, leading to dehydration and health issues.

Without regular veterinary care, stray Buji suffer from untreated infections, parasites, and injuries. They are also at higher risk of contracting and spreading diseases like FeLV and FIV.

All animals adopted from HAS are or will be sterilised for free at the ABC and Rescue Centre. It is mandatory and entirely free of cost for the adopter. Buji will be sterilised at the appropriate time.
