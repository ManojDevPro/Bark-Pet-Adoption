SUBURAMANI

In silence, I found strength, and in love, I discovered my second chance!

Name
Subu

Gender
Male

Energy Level
Low

Size
Medium

Meet Subu, an incredibly calm and quiet canine who was found abandoned along with his sibling. Sharing a strong bond, the pair navigated the cruel streets together, until fate dealt them an even crueller hand. Subu’s beloved sibling died, leaving behind a void that could clearly be seen in the depths of his expressive eyes. Ever since then, this gentle dog’s trust in humans has waned, forcing him to become withdrawn and seek solace in quiet corners, hidden away from the world.

Under our constant care, however, Christy continues to transform into a dog who wants to enjoy life, and who wants to share it with someone special. His quiet nature carries a profound wisdom, and if you have space in your heart and home for a bruised spirit seeking the healing power of love, consider giving dear Subu a home.

All animals adopted from HAS are or will be sterilised for free at the ABC and Rescue Centre. It is mandatory and entirely free of cost for the adopter. Christy's sterilised.
