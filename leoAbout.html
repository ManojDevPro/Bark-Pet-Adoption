LEO

One of life’s hurdles tripped me over at a young age, but I soon bounced back!

Name
Leo

Gender
Male

Energy Level
Medium

Size
Medium

Leo's life had a tumultuous start; facing adversity when he was just 45 days old. A tragic accident left his tiny paws crushed, and for two long days, little Andrew endured his painful plight on the unforgiving streets, unnoticed and uncared for by the world around him. Finally, someone noticed him and initiated treatment for this brave young soul. Working tirelessly to nurse Andrew back to health, the team spent many days and weeks helping him recover and get used to life with three legs. They needn’t have worried – this little lad was back on his paws and thriving in next to no time!

What sets Leo apart is his exceptional ability to revel in life, and while he may be a tri-pawd, he flourishes and thrives in every moment. With extreme loyalty and keen vigilance, this beautiful boy would be a priceless addition to any loving household. So, if you're in search of a companion who can infuse your life with immeasurable love and unbridled joy, Leo may well fit the bill.

All animals adopted from HAS are or will be sterilised for free at the ABC and Rescue Centre. It is mandatory and entirely free of cost for the adopter. Leo's sterilised.
