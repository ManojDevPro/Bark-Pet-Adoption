MUTHUPANDI

I’m ‘hank’ering for a loving home; that’s not too much to ask for, is it?

Name
Buddy

Gender
Male

Energy Level
High

Size
Medium

Muthu faced adversity right from the get-go when as an abandoned pet, residents in the area, rather than offering compassion and care, subjected him to abject cruelty. Stones were hurled, water was thrown – and poor muthu bore both the physical and emotional scars of this torment when finally rescued.

Covered in ticks and utterly downhearted, his condition served as a poignant reminder of the cruelty of humans. Fortunately for Hank, following a course of intensive treatment and lots of tender care, he has put that troubled time in his life firmly in the past where it belongs, and his trust in humanity has been slowly rekindled.

Hank's personality blends friendliness, playfulness, and a zest for life. He will thrive under the care of a family experienced with his breed, and one that can provide ample space for his exuberance. As a single pet, he seeks to rewrite his past; will you be the one to pen a new chapter in Muthu's life?
