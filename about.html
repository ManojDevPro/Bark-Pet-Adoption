About Us

Introduction

Welcome to Bark, where we believe that every pet deserves a loving and forever home. Our organization is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming pets who are in need. With a passionate team of volunteers and staff, we provide comprehensive care and support to animals while advocating for their welfare and promoting responsible pet ownership within the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets in need. We believe every animal deserves a second chance and we work tirelessly to make that happen.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every pet is cherished and cared for, and where communities are engaged and educated about the importance of animal welfare. Our goal is to create a network of support for pets and their owners, fostering a compassionate and humane society.
